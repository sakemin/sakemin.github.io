Pormoku

2016

Game

Processing, Unity

with Youngseo Kim

Background Music by Bonsung Koo

Direction, Development, Design : Jongmin Jung

Video : Youngseo Kim

A game based on oriental traditional game, 'gomoku'(five-in-a-row). You must make your stones five in a row first. The basic rule is same with gomoku, but in this game, there are 'portals'. You make your stones five in a row through portals, too.
Inspired by the game 'Portal' by Valve.
